What we do

Full-service yard care, done right.

Weekly or one-time, here's everything we handle — and exactly what's included. No contracts, no upsells, a fair price before we start.

1

Weekly mowing

A clean, even cut every visit — at the right height for the season so your lawn stays healthy, not just short.

  • Cut at the right height for the time of year
  • Edged along walks, drives & beds
  • String-trimmed around fences, trees & posts
  • Clippings blown off every hard surface
  • Weekly or bi-weekly — your schedule
2

Trimming & edging

The details that make a yard look finished instead of just mowed. Crisp lines, every visit.

  • Sharp edges along every walkway and driveway
  • Clean borders around beds and trees
  • Straight, intentional lines — not freehand
  • Done every visit, not once a month
3

Weeding & beds

Beds are what separate a kept yard from a cut one. We keep yours tidy and clean.

  • Hand-pulled weeds in beds and borders
  • Fresh, tidy bed lines
  • Mulch top-ups on request
  • Beds that look maintained, not overgrown
4

Hedges & shrubs

Shaped and balanced — never hacked, never lopsided. We treat them like they're our own.

  • Shaped and balanced for clean form
  • Seasonal shaping that keeps growth healthy
  • Clippings cleaned up and hauled off
  • Shrubs kept tidy and intentional
5

Sprinkler service

Stratton's got a real foundation in sprinkler systems, so your whole yard gets the water it needs — evenly.

  • Spring start-ups & full system checks
  • Leak and broken-head repairs
  • Adjustments for full, even coverage
  • Fall blow-outs before the freeze
6

Seasonal clean-ups

Spring and fall resets — or any one-time job to get the yard back in shape. No contract needed.

  • Spring resets to wake the yard up
  • Fall leaf & debris removal
  • One-time jobs welcome — no commitment
  • Great before a sale, party, or move-in
